MAGMASOFT® Modules

The platform, module by module.

MAGMASOFT® is the most widely used casting simulation platform — and the core of how we work. We choose the modules around your question, not the other way around.

M / 01

Filling

Understand how molten metal moves through gating and cavity. Air entrainment, cold shuts, and misruns identified before the first trial.

M / 02

Solidification

Locate hot spots, shrinkage risk, feeder performance, and porosity formation before the process reaches production.

M / 03

Stress

Predict residual stress, distortion, and crack risk — so geometry, machining behavior, and fatigue can be considered earlier.

M / 04

Optimization

DoE-based virtual experiments compare alternatives and find robust process windows — without any physical trials.

Process

From first question to stable production.

Step 01

Listen

We start with your process, your part, and the decision you need to make.

Step 02

Model
